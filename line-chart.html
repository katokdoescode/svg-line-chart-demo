SVG TimeLine Chart

To mimic the Bezier function, intermediate points are added that participate in the calculation and are hidden via CSS.

If you want, you can display all points at once, no problem.

Such a graph is easy to modify and animate, it can be embedded anywhere.

If you remove the background and the line, you can throw away all JavaScript, the chart will remain adaptive.

Unfortunately, I couldn't make adaptive lines without JS, maybe someone will have some ideas about it, I will be glad to discuss!
